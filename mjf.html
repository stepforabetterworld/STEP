What is MJF?

MJF (Melvin Jones Fellow): The Melvin Jones Fellowship (MJF) is is a way for donors to extend their commitment to LCIF. There are 54 recognition levels beyond the initial US$1,000 MJF contribution with a unique pin for each given level. After contributing US$100,000, donors become Humanitarian Partners. Members raise $2,000 or more and get recognized at each $1,000 milestone as a PJMF recipent

What is PMJF?

PMJF (Progressive Melvin Jones Fellow): The Progressive Melvin Jones Fellowship (PMJF) is one of the highest honors given by STEP For A Better World LEO Club’s affiliate organization, Lions Club International towards the *Lions Clubs International Foundation (LCIF)*. It recognizes individuals for their dedication to humanitarian service and their contributions to the mission of serving communities worldwide. To become an MJF, a contribution of*$1,000 or more is made to LCIF which supports global humanitarian projects like vision care, poverty, hunger, diabetes, cancer, environment, disaster relief, and youth programs. The award is named after *Melvin Jones*, the founder of Lions Clubs International. If you are interested in starting your journey to raise a MJF or PJMF, please email stepforabetterworld@gmail.com
